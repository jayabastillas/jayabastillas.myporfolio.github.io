  • LAVIDATECH

    History

    La Vida Tech Solutions was established primarily to service a niche industry in software development. It seeks to establish itself as the premiere security-first and security consious software consulting and development firm in the Visayas, if not the Philippines.

    As of present time, it is developing its own product and training key people to develop and drive the business.

    Nature of Business

    La Vida Tech Solutions is a software consulting and development company. It provides consulting work for software development project and also produces software specified by clients from various industries.

    It also provides application security testing and network testing in the form of penetration testing.

    Process

    At La Vida Tech Solutions , We strive to understand what the client's perspective is on the consulting and development projects they seek our expertise with. As such, the process necessary to achieve such custom and personalized service is the adoption of agile practices. No specific agile practice is currently preferred but to name a few, we practice; Scrum, Extreme Programming, Lean Development, and Feature Driven Development.

    Management & Consulting Division

    The Management & Consulting has more than 18 years of combined experience at the heart of it. Its duty involves attracting clients and solving the problemss clients have with respect to software or software consulting need, consequently La Vida Tech Solutions can develop the software necessary shoul available/recommended existing software is not to the client's specifications.

    Software Development Division

    The Software Development Division is our customized software solution department and our porduct development arm. It seeks agile individuals who Operating System and/or Platform Agnostics who can adapt to the ever changing landscape of a technology Driven society. The division works with technology stack apt for the client's requirements, it could be a desktop solution for Windows or Linux with which C# .NET or QT/Java might be used respectively. It could be a web-based application with a variety of development stacks can be used. like, LAMPP, MEAN, etc.

    Organizational Structure

    The company is headed by Raymond Olavides in the Consulting & Software Development with Project ManageMent & Consulting of Mercedes Romualdez-Olavides.

    OJT Duty

    The OJT's were made to be acquainted with general purpose tools used for web-based application development. On an almost daily basis, new tools and development roadblocks are added to their stacks for them to use and work-on, Their Duty is to Think like and become software developers & solutions provider.

    Part of their Duty is to learn and understand why certain security-consious solutions is favored over a simpler implementation. The Desired effect is that they to learn how to think like a programmer and solve problems that concerns project stakeholders' needs without sacrificing application security and data integrity.

    The company seeks to hire fresh graduates but finds them lacking in technical skill and critical thinking necessary in a consulting and development service field. Tools and skillset the thinking necessary in a consulting and development service field. The tools and skillset the college graduates acquires at school is often lacking, misunderstood, or misused.

    The company mitigates the concern by accepting training partneship with academic institutions and giving ample time for On-the-Job Trainees to get acquainted with industry tools and onculcates in them indusstry based perspectives in providing solutions. It gives ample time for On-the-Job Trainee to grasp the tools & snippest of code assigned to them and evaluate them if they have understood how to utilize it in a production scenario.
